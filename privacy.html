Privacy Policy

AiZenPay Inc.  ·  Last Updated: April 2026  ·  Effective: April 2026

AiZenPay Inc. ("AiZenPay," "we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you visit our website at aizenpay.com or submit information through our forms.

Please read this policy carefully. If you do not agree with its terms, please discontinue use of the site.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you complete a form on our website (such as a merchant application or contact form), we collect:

  • Name, business name, and job title
  • Email address and phone number
  • Business type, industry vertical, and processing volume estimates
  • Website URL and any comments or messages you submit

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our site, we may automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • IP address and general geographic location
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring URL

In Phase 1, we use only essential cookies required for the site to function correctly. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries and process your merchant or partner applications
  • To contact you about your account, application status, or services you requested
  • To provide customer and technical support
  • To send you information about AiZenPay products and services you have expressed interest in
  • To improve our website and the services we offer
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

3. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: We may share information with vendors who assist us in operating our business (e.g., form processing via Netlify, email delivery). These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for services they provide to us.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

Our website uses essential cookies and browser local storage solely to:

  • Remember your cookie consent preference
  • Ensure core website functionality operates correctly

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies in Phase 1. If this changes in future versions of the site, this policy will be updated and your consent will be re-requested.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our website does not process payment card data — all payment processing occurs through AiZenPay's secure backend systems, which are PCI DSS Level 1 compliant.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information submitted through our website forms for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry, manage your merchant or partner relationship, and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us at privacy@aizenpay.com.

7. Your Rights (CCPA & General)

Depending on your state of residence, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to know what personal information we have collected about you
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information
  • The right to opt out of the sale of your personal information (note: we do not sell personal information)
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@aizenpay.com. We will respond to verifiable requests within 45 days.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies separately.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@aizenpay.com
  • General: info@aizenpay.com
  • Website: aizenpay.com/contact
