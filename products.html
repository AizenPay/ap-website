Card-Present Processing

In-Person Payments, Engineered for the Enterprise

Every swipe, dip, and tap — processed with AI risk scoring in real time. Terminal-agnostic architecture means you're never locked into proprietary hardware.

EMV Chip & PIN
Full EMV chip-and-PIN processing with hardware-level encryption. Meets global card brand standards and shifts liability away from your business.
NFC & Contactless
Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay, and all NFC-enabled cards. Tap-to-pay ready across every terminal in your estate.
Mobile POS
Accept payments anywhere on iOS or Android. Bluetooth card readers, QR codes, and full mPOS functionality for field teams and pop-up retail.
Terminal-Agnostic
Works with Ingenico, Verifone, PAX, Dejavoo, and other major terminal manufacturers. Bring your existing hardware or deploy fresh.
Magnetic Stripe Fallback
Full MSR support ensures legacy cards are never rejected at the point of sale. Graceful fallback with EMV preference enforced.
Real-Time AI Risk Scoring
Every transaction scored for fraud risk before authorization response. Machine learning models trained on billions of payment data points.

Intelligence Layer

AI Built Into Every Transaction

Our intelligence layer is not a bolt-on. It's the core of every authorization decision we make.

Real-Time Risk Scoring
Each transaction scored in <50ms using multi-dimensional models: device fingerprint, behavioral velocity, geographic signals, and historical merchant patterns.
Authorization Routing Optimization
Dynamic routing selects the optimal acquiring path for each transaction — maximizing approval probability and minimizing interchange cost simultaneously.
Chargeback Prediction & Suppression
ML models identify high-risk transactions before they become chargebacks. Automated pre-dispute response workflows reduce representment labor by up to 70%.
Behavioral Anomaly Detection
Continuous monitoring for velocity spikes, account takeover signals, and synthetic identity patterns. Alerts surface before losses occur.
Security & Compliance

Your Compliance Shield. Our Responsibility.

Merchants and partners operating with AiZenPay process under our compliance infrastructure. We carry the certification burden so you don't have to.

PCI DSS Level 1
The highest level of PCI compliance. Our infrastructure, your protection. Partners and merchants inherit our compliance posture for card data in scope.
Network Tokenization
Card network tokenization (Visa Token Service, Mastercard MDES) replaces raw PANs at the network level — reducing fraud and improving authorization rates.
End-to-End Encryption
P2PE-ready hardware encryption from swipe to processor. Card data is unreadable at every point in the transaction chain outside our secure environment.
EMV 3DS Compliance
Full EMVCo 3DS 2.x compliance for card-not-present transactions. Frictionless authentication for low-risk, step-up only when needed.
SOC 2 Type II Roadmap
SOC 2 Type II audit is on our 12-month roadmap. Enterprise and regulated-industry customers can expect full attestation in 2027.
